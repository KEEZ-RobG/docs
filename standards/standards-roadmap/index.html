Standards Roadmap

Introductionâ€‹

As mentioned in the LSPs Foundation section, the LSPs are not order dependant meaning a standard could use another one that comes after or before in order. For example LSP0 - ERC725Account uses LSP1 - UniversalReceiver, LSP2 - ERC725YJSONSchema and could use LSP3 - UniversalProfileMetadata, etc.

LSPs Roadmapâ€‹

In order to understand the LSPs properly, they should be studied in the following order:

  • ERC725 - General Executor and Data Key/Value Store

  • LSP2 - ERC725YJSONSchema

  • LSP1 - UniversalReceiver

  • LSP0 - ERC725Account

  • LSP6 - KeyManager

  • LSP3 - UniversalProfileMetadata

  • LSP7 - DigitalAsset

  • LSP8 - IdentifiableDigitalAsset

  • LSP4 - DigitalAsset-Metadata

  • LSP5 - ReceivedAssets

  • LSP9 - Vault

  • LSP10 - ReceivedVaults

  • LSP12 - IssuedAssets
